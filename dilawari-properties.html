Our Story

25 Years of Serving Delhi with Integrity

Founded in 1998 by Mr. Dilawari in the heart of Paharganj, Dilawari Properties was built on one simple principle: honest dealings, happy clients. Over two and a half decades, we've grown from a small local office into one of Delhi's most respected property consultancies.
